Vegan and integral pastry and bakery course.

This course is designed for people who want to take care of their health with a natural and organic diet, it also helps a lot in the diet of people with diabetes or gluten intolerance. Among the different things you will learn are the following: -Different protein flours, such as chickpea flour, coconut, almond, oatmeal, buckwheat, rye. -Produce oils that are essential for good nutrition. -The use of natural sweeteners, such as powdered or liquid stevia -Different types of recipes and preparation of vegan and wholemeal meals and desserts.
